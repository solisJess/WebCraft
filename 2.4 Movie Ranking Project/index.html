This show was good and was great to see Pedro Pascal in it. I do think

The White Lotus, Season 2

The second season is a lot better to me than the first season was. I don't
know if it was because of the cast and the fact that I love Aubrey Plaza
but I thought that the storyline of the second season had a lot of depth.
